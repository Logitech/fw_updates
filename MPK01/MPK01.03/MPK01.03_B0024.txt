MPK01.03 Build 0024
-------------------

DFU for Logitech K780 Multi-Device Wireless Keyboards with firmware MPK01.xx.

Firmware
 - Name: MPK01
   Version: 03
   Unifying identifier: 405B

Boot-loader
 - Name: BOT25
   Version: any

Modification(s)
 - Fix compatibility with Unifying receivers (dongles) RQR12.xx (xx >= 08)
   and RQR24.xx (xx >= 06).
