RQR44.01 Build 0005
-------------------

DFU for the Logitech Lightspeed Receiver (dongle) with firmware RQR44.xx.

Firmware
 - Name: RQR44
   Version: 01
   USB identifier: 046D/C53F/4401 (Vendor id./Product id./BCD release)

Boot-loader
 - Name: BOT72
   Version: any
   USB identifier: 046D/AADE/72yy (Vendor id./Product id./BCD release)

Modification(s)
 - Address CVE-2019-13055
   https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2019-13055
 - Miscellaneous bug fixes.
