
Release notes
=============

RQK63.02 Build 0016
-------------------

DFU for Logitech Wireless Touch Keyboards K400 Plus with firmware RQK63.xx.

Firmware
 - Name: RQK63
   Version: 02
   Unifying identifier: 404D

Boot-loader
 - Name: BOT22
   Version: any

Modification(s)
 - Fix compatibility with Unifying receivers (dongles) RQR12.xx (xx >= 08)
   and RQR24.xx (xx >= 06).
