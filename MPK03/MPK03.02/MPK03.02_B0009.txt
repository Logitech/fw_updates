
Release notes
=============

MPK03.02 Build 0009
-------------------

DFU for the Logitech K370s or K375s Multi-Device Wireless Keyboard with
firmware MPK03.xx.

Firmware
 - Name: MPK03
   Version: 02
   Unifying identifier: 4061

Boot-loader
 - Name: BOT42
   Version: any
   Unifying identifier: 4061

Modification(s)
 - Fix compatibility with Unifying receivers (dongles) RQR12.xx (xx >= 08)
   and RQR24.xx (xx >= 06).
