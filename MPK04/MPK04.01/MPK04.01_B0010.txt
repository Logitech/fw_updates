MPK04.01 Build 0010

DFU for Logitech K850 Performance Wireless Keyboards with firmware MPK04.xx.

Firmware
 - Name: MPK04
   Version: 01
   Unifying identifier: 4062

Boot-loader
 - Name: BOT43
   Version: any

Modification(s)
 - Fix compatibility with Unifying receivers (dongles) RQR12.xx (xx >= 08)
   and RQR24.xx (xx >= 06).
